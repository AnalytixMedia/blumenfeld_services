## Research Findings

### Fonts (extracted from Wix source HTML)
| Role | Original Wix | V3 Match (Google Fonts) |

| Primary headings | `Syne` | `Syne` (exact match, free on Google Fonts) |

| Bold body | `avenir-lt-w01_85-heavy` | `DM Sans` weight 600 |
